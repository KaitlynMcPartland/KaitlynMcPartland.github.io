About me.


Kaitlyn McPartland

I’m a web developer with a background in project management and healthcare. While working my way up at a leading EMR company, I ended up overseeing teams of 60+ people while managing multimillion dollar projects.

After working with technical professionals for a few years, I realized I also had a desire to build and create. I enrolled in Dev Bootcamp where I spent 1000+ hours coding, pair programming and learning agile development methodologies.

I am driven by my ability to hone in on problems and create solutions that improve product outcomes and human lives. I’m currently seeking an entry level software development role in a passionate, efficient team.
